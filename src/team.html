Vision Statement

The Aben Team's vision is to be the leader in real estate for Muskoka by providing exemplary personalized service that exceeds current industry practices.

Mission Statement

To provide ethical and honest real estate services to our clients listing or selling Muskoka real estate.

Latest Technologies

The Aben Team will always incorporate the latest technologies in the marketing, listing and selling of Muskoka luxury homes and Muskoka cottage properties.

Latest Analysis & Resources

The Aben Team continually strives to provide the most current analysis and resources available to our clients, whose needs will always come first in each and every transaction.

Investment in Community

The Aben Team will continue to stay invested in our Muskoka community by contributing our time and financial resources.

John Aben has been serving the community of Muskoka for over 25 years. The local expertise he’s able to offer his clients is unmatched; he is the complete package when it comes to your property needs. Chairman’s Club Award recipient for top 1% sales in Canada, multiple year recipient of the Diamond Award for the 3% sales in Canada and Huntsville Forester Reader’s Choice Award for Best Realtor. John has taken his knowledge and love for technology and put it into his real estate selling techniques

The lifestyle quality offered from recreational to healthcare to education make John passionate about life in Muskoka. When he gets a few free moments, he loves spending time with his wife and family. Other interests include golf, skiing, cycling, fishing and camping.

Mark Aben has a vast range of knowledge in sales and customer service after spending close to 20 years as an owner in a successful marketing company. This, combined with growing up in Muskoka, having the experience of family cottaging and knowing the value of tradition has allowed Mark to understand the importance behind buying or selling a piece of Muskoka. He brings integrity, honesty, hard work and creativity to every real estate transaction.

When he’s not out searching for the perfect home or cottage for his clients, Mark is a dedicated father; actively involved with his three-boy’s passion for hockey. He also enjoys boating, water skiing and spending time with his wife of 20 years.
